Barack Obama, 44th President of the United States

Timeline of President Obama's life

  • 1961-Barack Hussein Obama II was born in Honolulu, Hawaii
  • 1981-Made his first public speech
  • 1983-Graduated from Columbia University
  • 1989-Met future wife Michelle Robinson
  • 1991-Graduated from Harvard Law School
  • 1992-Married his wife Michelle
  • 1996-Elected to the Illinois Senate
  • 1998-First daughter Malia was born
  • 2001-Second daughter Natasha("Sasha") was born
  • 2003-Became the chairman of the Illinois Senate's Health and Human Services Committee
  • 2004-Delivered the keynote address at the Democratic National Convention
  • 2004-Elected to the United States Senate
  • 2007-Announced his candidacy for President of the United States
  • 2008-Elected the 44th President of the United States
  • 2010-Signed the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act into law
  • 2012-Reelected the President of the United States
